Country Name & Meaning

Avenger Island is named after heroes who help people. The name reminds us to be brave and kind.

Capital CityStark City (east coast)
Motto“Stronger Together.”
CurrencyStark Shekels (STK) — the money used in shops and on trains.
National AnimalPanthera — fast, quiet and protective.

Why these Symbols?

The lightning is for Thor. The blue circle is for Iron Man’s power. The star is for Captain America. The colours red, blue and gold show hope and courage.

Culture

People try to be brave, kind and fair like the Avengers. Kids like to dress up as their favourite heroes. There are parades with music and fireworks.

Language

People on Avenger Island speak English. They sometimes use hero words like “assemble” when they want everyone to come together, or “smash it” to wish someone good luck.

Food

People eat shawarma (like in the movie), bagels, fruit from the farms, and honey cakes from New Asgard. Families share big meals on weekends.

Clothing & Celebrations

Everyday clothes are comfy with red, blue and gold. On special days people wear capes and star pins. The biggest party is Founders’ Day with lanterns shaped like shields, hammers, webs and panthers.

Climate

The north is cold and sometimes snowy. The middle is sunny and good for farms. The south is warm and tropical with beaches.

Natural Features

There is Hulk Ridge (big mountains), the Heartstream River, two bright Unity Lakes, the Panthera Forest, and the tall Asgard Cliffs.

Where People Live

Most people live in Stark City near schools and hospitals. Some live at New Asgard Harbor to fish. Rangers live near the Panthera Preserve to look after animals.

Need 1: Energy

Our Solution

We use clean power from sun, wind and waves. Big batteries near the lakes store extra power. This idea was inspired by Iron Man and Black Panther.

Need 2: Transport

Our Solution

StormRail is a fast train that goes from the mountains to the sea. In towns there are WebWays (safe bridges and bike paths) that look like Spider-Man’s webs. This helps everyone get to school, markets and beaches.

How Geography Helps Us

The river and lakes give us water and power, so towns are close to them. The warm south is good for beaches and fruit. The flat middle makes it easy to build the train.

Hero Inspiration

Hulk = strength, Spider-Man = helping neighbours, Iron Man = inventions, Thor = courage, Captain America = fairness, Black Panther = caring for nature.
