🏳️ Country Name & Meaning

Avenger Island is named after heroes who help people. The name reminds us to be brave and kind. 🛡️❤️

🏙️ Capital CityStark City (east coast)
🗣️ Motto“Stronger Together.” 🤝
💰 CurrencyStark Shekels (STK) — the money used in shops and on trains. 🚆
🐾 National AnimalPanthera — fast, quiet and protective.
National Flag 🏳️

🔰 Why these Symbols?

⚡ The lightning is for Thor. 🔵 The blue circle is for Iron Man’s power. ⭐ The star is for Captain America. 🎨 The colours red, blue and gold show hope and courage.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Culture

People try to be brave, kind and fair like the Avengers. 🦸‍♂️ Kids dress up as their favourite heroes. 🎆 There are parades with music and fireworks.

🗣️ Language

People speak English. They use hero words like “assemble” to call everyone together, or “smash it” for good luck. 💬

🍽️ Food

Shawarma (team favourite) 🌯, bagels 🥯, fruit from farms 🍎🍌, and honey cakes from New Asgard 🍰. Families share big meals on weekends. 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

👕 Clothing & 🎉 Celebrations

Everyday clothes are comfy with red, blue and gold. 🧥 On special days people wear capes and star pins. 🦸 The biggest party is Founders’ Day with lanterns shaped like shields 🛡️, hammers ⚒️, webs 🕸️ and panthers 🐾.

🌦️ Climate

North = cold and sometimes snowy ❄️. Middle = sunny and good for farms 🌾. South = warm, tropical and beachy 🏖️.

🏞️ Natural Features

Hulk Ridge ⛰️ (big mountains in the west), Jotun Peaks 🧊 (snowy tops in the north), Panthera Hills 🐾 (green hills in the east), and the tall Asgard Cliffs ⛰️ (at the south). The Heartstream River 💧 runs through the middle

🏘️ Where People Live

Most people live in Stark City 🏙️ near schools and hospitals. Some live at New Asgard Harbor ⚓ to fish. Rangers live near the Panthera Preserve 🐾 to look after animals.

Infrastructure & Design

How our country runs: energy ⚡ and transport 🚆.

🔌 Energy

People get power from the sun ☀️, wind 🌬️ and water 🌊. Big batteries 🔋 keep the lights on at night. Iron Man 🤖 thought of the idea, and Black Panther 🐾 helped make it safe for nature.

🚆 Transport

StormRail is a fast train that goes across the island. Thor ⚡ opened it with a lightning show. In towns there are WebWays 🕸️ (safe bridges and paths). Spider-Man 🕷️ showed people how to build them. Captain America 🛡️ made sure everyone can use them fairly, and Hulk 💪 lifted the heavy parts.
